Who I am

Born and raised on Kauai, HI. I am Kyle Kaimana Bulloch. I have always been fascinated by how things work. From the smallest details of physics and chemistry to the big questions about the structure of the universe and the nature of consciousness, it can all be reduced to a set of logical rules. Writing code tickles my brain in just the right way, because it is deriving pure logic from a problem. I love creating something out of nothing, adding to the entropy of the universe. I love how writing code allows you to do just about anything you can imagine, building a world of self-reliant rules that the programmer defines. The possibilities are endless and I want to explore those unknown spaces. I am an aspiring Full Stack Web Developer recently graduated from Epicodus.

I just finished a two month internship at Copious where I helped develop a suite of automated tests for a large ecommerce site. I learned a lot about the backend of the Magento ecommerce CMS. I wrote backend functional tests with PHPSpec and front end web tests with Behat for a few dozen different use cases.
